Teacher workflow

Create. Assign. Monitor. Mark.

From writing the exam to handing back grades — entirely in the browser. No printing, no USB drives, no emailing zip files. One shared lab, pushed to every student at once.

1

Create the assignment or exam

Build a task in minutes — pick a lab template, set questions, define a time limit and choose which tools students have access to. Labs can be network topologies, coding challenges or web projects.

2

Push to the class — one click

Select a class, hit deploy. Every student's workstation is provisioned instantly with an isolated clone of the lab. Students receive a notification and log in with their school credentials from any device.

3

Monitor the room live

Watch every student's progress in real time. See who's connected, how far through the task they are, and flag anyone who needs help. Drop into any workstation to observe — without interrupting the student.

4

Mark remotely — anytime

Once submitted, review each student's lab state, terminal history and output logs. Add scores and annotations from anywhere. Grades sync back to the student portal automatically.

Secure student access

Students log in.
You stay in control.

Each student gets their own credentials issued by the school. They log in from home, from school, from any thin PC or Chromebook — and land directly in their isolated sandbox. No setup, no confusion.

School-issued credentials

Each student gets a unique username and password provisioned by the teacher. No personal accounts, no third-party sign-in — fully under school control.

Access from any device, anywhere

Students work from school, home, the library — any browser, any OS. Thin PCs and Chromebooks connect at full speed because compute runs in the cloud lab, not on the device.

Exam lockdown mode

During a timed exam, each student's sandbox is isolated — no access to other students' labs, restricted external internet, and all actions are logged for the marking record.

Teacher observes at any time

Drop into any student's workstation silently to see exactly what they're doing, without interrupting their session. Jump in to assist or simply observe for integrity.
