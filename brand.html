07 The dot - round, never square

Geist's period glyph is squarish. So the wordmark's dot is not the text character - it's a real circle element, baseline-aligned, sized in em so it scales with the type. In code: <span class="wm">netplex<span class="dot"></span></span> with .dot{border-radius:50%}.
