Privacy Policy

netplex is built privacy-first. We collect the minimum needed to run the service, and we never collect the contents of your labs.

What we collect

  • Account data - email and authentication identity (via OIDC) to sign you in.
  • Failure telemetry, only with consent - when an image or topology fails, and only if you opt in: the failure type, image name + sha[:12], vendor/class/emulator, QEMU args (no configs or passwords), exit code, last 50 lines of stderr, and host CPU/RAM/disk.

What we never collect

  • Your identity tied to lab contents, your configurations, passwords, or memory/disk contents.
  • Anything at all if you decline telemetry - the "send diagnostics" buttons disappear for good.

Your rights

Export or delete your data at any time. Enterprise customers can choose data residency. We honour GDPR and CCPA rights; sub-processors are listed in the in-app DPA.

Open-source notices

netplex stands on the shoulders of excellent open-source projects, used in compliance with their licences and kept as separate processes/services:
