Questions

Pricing, answered.

Is the free tier time-limited?

No — free is free forever. 25 nodes and 3 labs is a genuinely useful ceiling, bigger than EVE Community's practical limit.

Monthly or annual?

Both. Annual gives you two months free. EVE Pro is annual-only and clunky — monthly is our wedge.

What counts as a "host"?

A physical or virtual machine running the Netplex compute agent. The distributed fabric stitches them together over VXLAN.

Do you cripple paid features?

Never. Every tier gets the full canvas, consoles and image pipeline. Paid unlocks scale, teams and automation — not basic usability.

Free for students?

Yes. Verify with a school email for free individual access. Institutes get steep per-seat classroom bundles.

Will licensing ever lock my running lab?

No. A licensing blip never hard-locks a running lab — there's a grace window. Reliability beats DRM zeal.
