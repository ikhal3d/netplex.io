Questions

Pricing, answered.

Is it really free for Australian schools?

Yes — for every Australian primary and secondary school. We install the software on your school's server and personally train your teachers, all at no cost. TAFEs and universities, please contact us for a quote. Email edu@netplex.io to get started.

Why pay when EVE-NG has a free Community?

You don't have to - netplex. Associate is also free, and already better. You pay for what EVE-NG can't sell you: snapshots, fault injection, presentation mode, Terraform, Ansible, QoS and a built-in metrics dashboard.

Is the Associate tier time-limited?

No - free is free forever. 20 nodes and a live lab is a genuinely useful ceiling for routing & switching certification study, and the full engine, every console and the built-in tool nodes are all included.

What's the difference between Professional and Architect?

Professional ($199/yr) is the solo "version it, break it, present it" tier - 1,000 nodes, snapshots, link/node fault injection, any-Docker and presentation mode. Architect ($299/yr) adds config-as-code & intent validation, QoS impairment (delay/jitter/loss), the automation toolbox (API, Terraform, Ansible), the built-in metrics dashboard and micro-VM/LXC. Team brings the people layer; Enterprise the org layer.

Why is there a 1 Mbps cap on Associate?

It's intentional, gentle friction. Routing and switching study doesn't need throughput; serious QoS or WAN simulation does - and that's a Professional/Architect conversation.

Will a licence blip lock my running lab?

No. Enterprise uses MAC + TPM fingerprinting with a 30-day grace period on hardware migration. Reliability beats DRM zeal.
