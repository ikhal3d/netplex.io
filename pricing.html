Questions

Pricing, answered.

Is it really free for Australian schools?

Yes — completely. netplex. is free for every school across Australia: primary, secondary, and TAFE. We install the software on your school's server and personally train your teachers, all at no cost. Email edu@netplex.io to get started.

Why pay when EVE-NG has a free Community?

You don't have to - netplex. Community is also free, and already better. You pay for what EVE-NG can't sell you: snapshots, git auto-backup, Terraform, Ansible, QoS simulation and cloud templates.

Is the Community tier time-limited?

No - free is free forever. 20 nodes and a live lab is a genuinely useful ceiling for routing & switching certification study.

What does Pro hold back?

Nothing. Pro is a single-seat licence, so there's no reason to cripple it - you get the complete automation toolbox. Enterprise adds the organisational layer (SSO, multi-tenant, audit, cluster).

Why is there a 10 Mbps cap on Community?

It's intentional, gentle friction. Routing and switching study doesn't need throughput; serious QoS or WAN simulation does - and that's a Plus/Pro conversation.

Will a licence blip lock my running lab?

No. Enterprise uses MAC + TPM fingerprinting with a 30-day grace period on hardware migration. Reliability beats DRM zeal.
